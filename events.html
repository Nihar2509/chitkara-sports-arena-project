Khelo India Event

Khelo India: Building a Strong Sporting Nation

India, an initiative launched by the Government of India, is a nationwide program aimed at transforming the sports ecosystem in the country. With a vision to create a strong sporting culture and harness the potential of young athletes, Khelo India focuses on identifying and nurturing talent from grassroots to elite levels.
The program promotes sports at both school and university levels, ensuring that the best talent gets the opportunity to receive professional training and showcase their skills on national and international platforms. The initiative emphasizes the importance of physical fitness and mental well-being while encouraging inclusivity by providing equal opportunities for all athletes, regardless of their background.For institutions like Chitkara University, Baddi, Khelo India offers immense potential to align with national sports initiatives,
encouraging students to actively participate and represent the university in various competitions. By fostering a sports culture on campus, the university can motivate its students to excel in their chosen fields, providing top-notch infrastructure and coaching to support their endeavors.Participating in Khelo India events would also enhance the university's reputation as a hub for athletic development.
