About Cricket at Chitkara

Cricket is one of the most popular sports at Chitkara University, attracting students who are passionate about the game. Our cricket program offers opportunities for students to develop their skills, engage in competitive play, and represent the university in regional and national tournaments.

Facilities

Our cricket facilities include a full-sized cricket ground with a well-maintained pitch, practice nets, and advanced equipment. The field is equipped with seating arrangements and is maintained to meet competitive standards, providing the best environment for our players.
