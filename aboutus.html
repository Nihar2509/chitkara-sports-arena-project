About the Sports Complex

The Chitkara University Sports Complex is a state-of-the-art facility that promotes physical fitness and sportsmanship among students. With a wide range of modern amenities, the complex is designed to encourage participation in sports, whether for recreation or competitive excellence. The complex caters to both indoor and outdoor sports, providing students with ample opportunities to develop their skills and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Sports Offered

  • Cricket, Football, Basketball, Tennis, Badminton, Table Tennis, Volleyball, Athletics & Indoor Sports (Chess, Carrom, etc.)

Facilities

Our sports complex includes:

  • Cricket Ground with practice nets and Football Field
  • Basketball and Tennis Courts
  • Indoor Sports Hall with Fully equipped Gymnasium
  • Track and Field Athletics Area
