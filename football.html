About Football at Chitkara

Football is one of the most celebrated sports at Chitkara University. Our football program is dedicated to fostering a competitive spirit, teamwork, and skill development among students. Players of all levels are welcome to join and participate in both recreational and competitive games.

Facilities

We have a professional-grade football field on campus, equipped with high-quality turf, lighting for evening matches, and ample seating for spectators. Our facilities are maintained to provide the best environment for training and tournaments.
