Rules of Chitkara University Sports Arena (Baddi)

General Rules

  • All students and staff must carry their ID cards to access the sports arena facilities.
  • Proper sports attire, including non-marking shoes, is mandatory for using the indoor sports facilities.
  • No food or beverages (except water) are allowed inside the sports complex.
  • Use of offensive language, aggressive behavior, or any form of harassment is strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking and alcohol consumption are not permitted in the arena and its surrounding areas.
  • Users must respect and maintain cleanliness of the facilities. Littering is prohibited.

Specific Rules for Different Sports

Basketball Court

  • Maximum playtime per group is 1 hour. Players must vacate the court after the session if others are waiting.
  • Only rubber-soled shoes are allowed on the basketball court.
  • Avoid excessive rough play to prevent injuries.

Football Ground

  • Matches must be booked in advance. Walk-in games are allowed only if the ground is free.
  • Only appropriate football boots are permitted on the field.
  • Players must be mindful of others and avoid unnecessary physical contact.

Badminton Court

  • Players should exhibit good sportsmanship and mutual respect at all times.
  • No food or drinks (except water) are allowed inside the badminton court area.
  • Use of proper badminton rackets and shuttlecocks is mandatory.
  • Players must wear appropriate sports attire, including non-marking badminton shoes, to avoid damaging the court surface.

Penalties for Rule Violation

  • Failure to comply with the rules may result in temporary or permanent suspension of access to the sports arena.
  • Damage to university property will result in penalties, including financial charges for repairs or replacements.
  • Serious offenses, such as harassment or substance abuse, will be dealt with by university authorities and could result in disciplinary action.
