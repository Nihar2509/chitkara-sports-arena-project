Badminton

Badminton at Chitkara University, Baddi

Badminton is a popular sport at Chitkara University, Baddi, providing students with a platform to stay fit, learn discipline, and compete in regional tournaments. Our university boasts top-notch badminton facilities, including multiple courts, professional training sessions, and regular practice timings.

Facilities and Timings

We have two well-maintained indoor badminton courts available for both beginners and advanced players. The courts are equipped with high-quality flooring and proper lighting to ensure the best playing experience. Timings for practice sessions are as follows:

  • Morning: 6:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Evening: 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Why to Join?

Whether you're looking to pursue badminton professionally or simply want a way to stay active, our badminton program is designed for you. Benefits of joining include:

  • Guidance from certified badminton coaches
  • Opportunities to participate in inter-university tournaments
  • Access to exclusive fitness and conditioning programs
