About Volleyball at Chitkara

At Chitkara University, volleyball is one of the most popular sports, offering students a chance to engage in competitive and recreational play. Our volleyball program is open to all students, regardless of skill level, represent the university in intercollege tournaments, and participate in skill development sessions.

Facilities

Our volleyball courts are designed to meet national standards, equipped with high-quality nets, flooring, and lighting for evening practices. We ensure a safe and supportive environment for students to hone their skills and enjoy the game.

Join Us

If interested in joining the volleyball team or participating in any related events, please visit the sports office or contact us via email .
