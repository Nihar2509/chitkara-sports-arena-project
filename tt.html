Table Tennis

About Table Tennis at Chitkara

At Chitkara University, table tennis is a popular indoor sport that provides students with opportunities for both competitive and recreational play. Our table tennis program welcomes players of all skill levels, offering intramural matches, and regular practice sessions for skill development.

Facilities

Our table tennis facilities include professional-grade tables, paddles, and balls, as well as a spacious area for singles and doubles matches. The setup meets all national standards, ensuring a high-quality playing experience for our students.

Join Us

If you're interested in joining the table tennis team or participating in related events, please visit the sports office or contact us via email.
